Finances

Since our project changed so much throughout our 4 sprints, our finances changed a lot as well. Throughout the project, we purchases many items, such as brushless motors, that we thought we would use, but ended up not using and returning. Our final design allowed us to use a lot of materials that were already provided to us; mainly the use of servos which tend to add up in cost when you have to purchase them individually.
