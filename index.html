About Complex Kinect

Complex Kinect is a team of 5 students at Olin College of Engineering, an innovative undergraduate engineering school located in Needham, MA. The goal of our project is integrate elements of mechanical, electrical and software design by constructing an interactive structure of nodes that responds to motion in its environment.

Learn more about our iterative process, check out the final system, read our blog or meet the team.
