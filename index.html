Welcome!

Complex Kinect is a team composed of five interdisciplinary engineering students focused on combining artistic elements and strong engineering skills to bring an interactive and creative experience for the user. We combine our software, electrical, and mechanical skills to create a kinetic sculpture that moves according to several inputs, ranging from audio to motion.

Our team currently attends Olin College of Engineering, a small engineering only school located in Needham, MA. Three members of our team study Engineering with Computing, and the other two members study Mechanical Engineering.

Learn more about our iterative process, check out the final system, read our blog or meet the team.
